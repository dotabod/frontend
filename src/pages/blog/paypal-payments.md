## PayPal just slid into the checkout

Big news for the wallet warriors. You can now pay for Dotabod Pro with PayPal. No card details to dig out of your drawer, just tap, log in, and you are Pro.

### How it works

1. Pick your Pro plan. Monthly, yearly, or the big lifetime one.
2. Choose PayPal at checkout.
3. Log into PayPal and confirm.
4. Your Pro unlocks the second the payment clears.

### The fine print, minus the boring lawyer voice

- **Monthly and yearly renew on their own.** PayPal handles the recurring charge so you never lapse mid game.
- **Lifetime is still one and done.** One PayPal payment, Pro forever. Gorgeous.
- **Already paying another way?** Nothing to do. Keep carrying your team.
