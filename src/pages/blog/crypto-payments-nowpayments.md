## We gave crypto checkout a glow up

Remember how you could pay for Dotabod Pro with crypto? Good news. We ripped out the old plumbing and dropped in a shiny new payment provider called NOWPayments. More coins to pick from, and payments that just work.

### What actually changed for you

Honestly? Almost nothing, and that's the point. The flow is the same:

1. Pick your Pro plan. Monthly, yearly, or the big lifetime one.
2. Flip the "Pay with Crypto" toggle at checkout.
3. Pay the invoice with whatever coin lives in your wallet.
4. Your Pro unlocks the moment the payment clears.

### The fine print, minus the boring lawyer voice

- **Crypto doesn't renew on its own.** We'll poke you with an invoice before your time runs out. No spooky charges while you sleep.
- **No free trial on crypto.** Want the trial? Pay with a card. We don't make the rules. Okay, we kind of do.
- **Lifetime is still one and done.** One crypto payment, Pro forever. Beautiful.

Already paying with crypto? You don't have to do a thing. Sit back, sip your drink, carry your team.
